About Us

Serving Hive company

A nutritionary diet prep and delivery company (a service provider establishment). The main goal of this company is to help those who need a diet plan for their medical or health-related problems. This company can also help various individuals and groups as well, which are listed in the following paragraphs. The work of this company is comparable with what a nutritionist provides to patients in hospitals, but it differs in two ways. First, the clientele, this company targets more customers than just patients. Secondly, unlike nutritionists, this company takes responsibility for preparing and delivering products on a monthly or weekly basis.
